SOMAFIX PU SEALANT MULTI PURPOSE CONSTRUCTION & AUTOMOTIVE (SAUSAGE) S751

Description:

A multi-purpose polyurethane adhesive and sealant for dual purposes with permanent flexibility. Once applied, it hardens by reacting with air moisture to form a high-performance, fast-curing sealant adhesive. Fast curing. Can be easily painted. Surface can be easily smoothed and leveled. Non-corrosive. A highly versatile product with good adhesion to many surfaces. Excellent weather and water resistance.

Features & Application Areas:

SOMAFIX PU Sealant Multi Purpose Construction Automotive is a powerful, versatile sealing adhesive for all tasks involving sealing and bonding. (For a variety of materials including all cement-based materials, bricks, ceramics, glass, metals, wood, porous surfaces, polyester, acrylic resin, and plastics): Window and door frames made of wood, metal or plastic, roofs, fillings and covers, flexible air sealing, fixing lightweight materials, containers, tanks and silos.

Usage Instructions:

Ensure the surface to be applied is dry and clean, free from dust and grease. Tape the sides of the surface with adhesive tape. Cut the tip of the cartridge and place it in the gun. Cut the tip of the nozzle and insert it into the cartridge. Apply the sealant and remove the tapes. Protect from water for 24 hours after application.

Safety:

Keep out of reach of children. Avoid contact with eyes. Prolonged exposure to vapors may cause respiratory harm. Store in a cool, dry place.

Shelf Life:

12 months. Production date is printed on the product.
