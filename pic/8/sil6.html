SOMAFIX PU MONTAGE CLEAR BOND S150

Description:

Transparent, moisture-curing, solvent-free polyurethane construction adhesive with excellent resistance to water, heat and weather conditions.

Application Features:

Various materials can be joined without nails or screws. Strong adhesive for wood products such as veneers, pressed wood, stair renovation, corner joints in metal doors, fire-resistant fiberboards and gypsum boards, moldings, window and door frames, plaster moldings, cornices, ceiling accessories, wooden cabinets, bonding and insulation of expanded polystyrene and polyurethane boards, windows, facades, stones, natural stones, bricks and ceramics, aluminum, stainless steel, galvanized steel, copper, PVC and reinforced plastic.

Usage Instructions:

Surfaces must be clean. Polyurethane adhesive can be pressed through the cartridge nozzle. The glue requires moisture to cure, so the adhesive should be applied in a bead on moisture-impervious surfaces, or they should be moistened before bonding. Keep out of reach of children.

Safety:

Keep out of reach of children. Avoid contact with eyes. Prolonged exposure to vapors may cause respiratory harm. Store in a cool, dry place.

Shelf Life:

12 months from production date. Production date is printed on the product.
